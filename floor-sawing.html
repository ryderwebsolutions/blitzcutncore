Accurate trenching and joint cutting

Floor sawing is the clean alternative to breaking: straight cuts to a controlled depth with minimal vibration and no random cracking — ideal for service trenches, expansion joints and slab sections that will be lifted and reinstated.

  • Husqvarna walk-behind diamond floor saws for residential and commercial slabs
  • Cutting depth from 50mm up to 500mm+
  • Drainage runs, cable ducts, gas mains, expansion joints
  • Road and tarmac cutting for utility works
